    💡 Khung cấu trúc Speaking

    PREP
    Point — State your main answer
    Reason — Give a reason why
    Example — Add a specific example
    Point — Restate/conclude
    Best for: Part 1 & Part 3 opinions
    STAR
    Situation — Set the scene
    Task — The challenge or goal
    Action — What you did
    Result — The outcome
    Best for: Part 2 narrative stories
    PPF
    Past — Historical context
    Present — Current situation
    Future — What may happen
    Best for: Part 3 trend questions
    TIP PHRASES
    Buying time: "That's an interesting question…"
    Agreeing: "I'd have to say…" / "To my mind…"
    Disagreeing: "I'm not entirely sure, but…"
    Elaborating: "What I mean is…" / "For instance…"
    Use naturally to sound fluent
